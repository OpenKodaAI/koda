# Code Of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We want the Koda community to be welcoming, respectful, technically rigorous, and safe for people of all backgrounds and experience levels.

## Expected Behavior

- be respectful in discussions, reviews, and support threads
- assume good intent while still giving direct, actionable feedback
- focus criticism on ideas, code, and behavior, not people
- help keep the project accessible to new contributors and operators

## Unacceptable Behavior

- harassment, intimidation, or discrimination
- personal attacks or deliberately demeaning language
- sharing private information without permission
- disruptive behavior that prevents healthy collaboration

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove content, close threads, or restrict participation when behavior harms the community or the project.

## Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, contact the maintainers privately with enough context for review.
